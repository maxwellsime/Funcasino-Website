Blackjack

Blackjack is our second most common game and is a firm favourite. Blackjack is an American traditional casino game which has a large amount of varients including games 21 and pontoon. Blackjack is a 'comparing card' game where players compare their final and to the dealer's cards, with the objective to get a higher combination of cards up to 21. Go over 21 with your card combination and you're bust! Meaning you'll have to wait until he next hand to play. Aces cost 1 or 11, while all picture cards cost 10. The best combination of cards you want to look out for will be 'Blackjack' a combination of either a picture card or 10 card and an ace which gives the player 21 instantly.

Betting in Blackjack is very simple however there is two chances in the game to double your original bet.

Double Down

Doubling down on your hand is commonly known as "buying a card", you pay the price of your bet for one more card and no more. This can be used to attempt to get high hands while your starting hand is low (frequently around 10 in attempt to get 21).

Split

Splitting your cards is only possible when you have a pair of cards the same number. This'll split your two cards into two hands for the price of your bet again. This'll be used frequently when you have a pair of good cards like aces or picture cards.

Betting: Players will bet on one or more boxes on the table. This will be the first stage of the game and the croupier will wait until all players have bet.

Playing: The dealer will go round the table giving each player the choice of how they want to play their hand. This usually consists of three options: Stand, not getting anymore cards and staying on the card combination that the player has; Hit/Card, getting another card from the dealer in attempt to beat the dealer's hand; Double down(See betting above). Sometimes splitting your cards is also an oppurunity (See betting above again).

Paying: Winners will be given double their bets while losers will have their bet taken away. However if a player has gotten 'Blackjack' (Picture Card/10 + An ace) then they'll be rewarded their bet twice, that is if they can beat the dealer's hand...
