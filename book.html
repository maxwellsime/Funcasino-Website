Fun Money

Since our funcasinos aren't based on real money bets we use "fun money". Players with fun money can come up to any of the tables and cash the paper money in for chips to start playing. Each player has '100 pounds' worth of fun money each however can recieve more from friends or other people at the event freely to continue playing at our tables.
